Admissions

Join one of the best senior schools focused on academic excellence and all-round development.

Eligibility

Students from recognized schools who meet the academic criteria and have a passion for learning are welcome to apply.

Admission Procedure

  • Fill the online application form.
  • Attend counseling or entrance interview (if required).
  • Submit necessary documents.
  • Pay admission fee to confirm your seat.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Passport size photographs
